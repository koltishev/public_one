Elementies is a Pokemon-inspired, play-to-earn game universe that uses the Solana blockchain.

The game will be available on PC, macOS, Android, and iOS. The game’s main characters are Elementies, NFT tokens used in-game.

Players will gain rewards in the form of $ELM and $BRP tokens by winning battles in various PvE and PvP game modes and participating in events.

Learn more in the:

WHITEPAPER

PvP and PvE Battles

Battle against other players or join boss raids with your friends and earn rewards

Breeding

Collect Breeding Potion from your enemies in battles. Use them for breeding new Elementies with unique traits

Marketplace

Trade your NFTs, lands, and items with global players on the Marketplace or utilize those assets in the Elementies ecosystem

Fuzzy Lands

Find a home for your Elementies. You will be able to buy a cave inside Fuzzy mountain where your little creatures can live in peace

DAO Governance

NFT holders will be able to stake their $ELM tokens, participate in governance voting, and receive other potential rewards

Community fund

Seventy percent of royalties will go to the community fund. Players and DAO members will join raffles and competitions to receive extra rewards from the treasury

Trade your NFTs on the Kuju Marketplace

Trade your Menties, lands, farmed materials, and other collectibles/crafted NFT items on the Kuju Marketplace with low fees and instant transactions thanks to integration with Solana Network.
